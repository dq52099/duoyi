## Completion Rule

The full goal is not complete unless `scripts/alignment_regression_gate.sh` and `scripts/validate_goal_closure.sh` both pass. Passing groups 1-7 is necessary but not sufficient because REQ-NOTIFY, REQ-WIDGET, and REQ-DEVICE require device evidence.

Groups `6/8 Flutter analyzer` and `7/8 debug APK build` are full-goal quality/build gates enforced through the alignment summary. They do not have separate product requirement rows, but the goal cannot close unless both groups pass.
